Who is Ștefan?

After over ten years of experience in domestic freight transport as a transport coordinator, I decided to pursue my passion for web development. I graduated from the Academy of Economic Studies in Bucharest, specializing in accounting and management informatics, and recently completed the Web Development course from the Step It Academy in Bucharest, specializing in front-end web development.

I started the Web Development course with web design concepts using the well-known Figma application and after that, we emphasized HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. In the final part of the course, I learned the basics of the Angular framework and the JavaScript library React.

As an aspiring web developer, I enjoy discovering and learning new programming concepts, identifying and solving problems in code, and aiming to produce clean and efficient code.

I am looking for entry-level or internship employment opportunities on the front-end web development side, whether remote or not. If there is a position available, I would love to hear from you.
